# HotPot
A simple site for Pokémon teambuilding.

# How it works:

You can sort recommendations using four different metrics to find Pokémon that match your needs:

1. Joint Probability: Find a meta pick that fits with your team: who are these Pokémon most commonly seen with?
2. Affinity: Probability adjusted by the Pokémon's overall popularity. Helps find uncommon Pokémon that fit well with your team.
3. Hybrid: A blend of the two above.
4. Phi: A correlation coefficient that finds matches that correlate will with your team.
